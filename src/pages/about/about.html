About

Ever felt you're missing what's going on at your masjid or near by masajids?

This app will get you the happenings from ICOR, ICOB, MAPS, ICOE, ICON, Madeena Masjid currently. It keeps you up to date with the events at these masajids. You may mark events as attending if you plan to and the app will add them to your calendar (you can opt as well). You may also RSVP to the events which need to be notified back to the host. If you wish to add an event/announcement to the list, please reach out to icordevs@gmail.com.
