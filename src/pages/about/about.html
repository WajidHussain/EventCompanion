About

This app will get you the events & announcements from different masajids on the eastside. You need not rely on any email subscriptions or whatsapp groups or keep looking at the masjid website. Just subscribe to the masjid's you want to be informed of.

You may mark events as attending if you plan to and the app will add them to your calendar (you can opt in). You can opt in to get notifications from the different masajids. You may also RSVP to the events which need to be notified back to the host. If you wish to add an event/announcement or a masjid to the list, please reach out to icordevs@gmail.com.
